Grammar & Vocabulary Notes: Units 01-04

Unit 01: Family & Similarity

Present Simple for facts. Use "Both...and" or "In common" to show similarities.

Unit 02: Shopping & Past

Past Simple for finished actions. Comparatives (better, cheaper) for value.

Unit 03: Nutrition & Experience

Present Perfect for experiences. Too/Enough to describe quantity/quality.

Unit 04: Jobs & Continuous

Present/Past Continuous for actions in progress. Work conditions: demanding, flexible.
