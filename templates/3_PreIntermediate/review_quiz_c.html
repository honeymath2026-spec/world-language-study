Grammar & Vocabulary Notes: Units 09-12

Unit 09: Past Perfect (had + p.p.)

Action before another past action. "The shark died after it had been caught."
Used with extinct, pollination, defy, impulse.

Unit 10: First Conditional (If + present, will)

Real future possibilities. "If he saves money, he will travel."
Past necessity: had to / could.

Unit 11: Second Conditional & Used to

Used to for past habits. Second conditional: If + past, ... would + verb.
"If I stayed in a hotel, my children wouldn't have freedom."

Unit 12: Already / Just / Yet / Still

Time adverbs with present perfect. "Police have already introduced fines. He hasn't recovered yet."

📖 6. READING COMPREHENSION (Main ideas from Units 09-12)
Passage 1 (Unit 09): In Milgram’s experiment, participants were told to give electric shocks to learners. Even though the learners screamed and complained about heart problems, 65% obeyed the scientist in a white coat.
