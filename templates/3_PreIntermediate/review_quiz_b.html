Grammar & Vocabulary Notes: Units 05-08

Unit 05: Relax & Predictions

Will/Won't for future predictions and decisions. Adverbs of probability (probably, definitely).

Unit 06: Home & Description

Prepositions of place. Using Relative Clauses (which, where, that) to describe places and things.

Unit 07: Mind, Body & Rules

Modal Verbs (must, have to, can't) for obligation and permission. Health vocabulary.

Unit 08: Travel & Plans

Present Continuous for fixed future arrangements. Going to for intentions and plans.
