Grammar & Vocabulary Notes: Beginner Units 05-08

Unit 05: It's my life!

Present Simple (I/you/they). Likes and dislikes. a/an. Languages & nationalities. Numbers & prices.

Unit 06: Every day

Present Simple (he/she/it). Adverbs of frequency (usually, sometimes, never). Time. Days of the week.

Unit 07: Places I like

Question words (what, where, who, why, when, how). This/That. It/Them. Adjectives. Can I...?

Unit 08: Where I live

There is/are. Any. Prepositions of place (in, on, under, next to). Directions.

1. PRESENT SIMPLE (Units 5-6)

3. He _____ goes to bed late. (not at any time)
4. I _____ drink coffee in the morning. (100%)
5. They _____ eat pizza for lunch. (about 50%)
