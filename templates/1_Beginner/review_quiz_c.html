Grammar & Vocabulary Notes: Beginner Units 09-12

Unit 09: Happy birthday!

was / were (Past of 'be'). was born / were born. Past Simple irregular verbs (go, see, buy, take, say, sell, think).

Unit 10: We had a good time!

Past Simple questions and negatives. Regular verbs with -ed. Did / didn't.

Unit 11: We can do it!

can / can't (Ability). Requests and offers with can. Questions with can.

Unit 12: Thank you very much!

want and would like. Polite requests. Food and drink vocabulary.
