Grammar & Vocabulary Notes: Beginner Units 01-04

Unit 01: Hello!

Verb 'to be' (am/is/are). Possessive adjectives (my, your). Numbers 1-10. Plurals with -s.

Unit 02: Your World

His/Her. He's/She's. Asking about names and origins. Numbers 11-30.

Unit 03: Personal Information

Verb 'be' negatives and questions. Jobs. Short answers. Wh-questions.

Unit 04: Family and Friends

Family vocabulary. Possessive adjectives (his, her, their). Have/Has.
