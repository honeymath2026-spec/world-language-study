Grammar & Vocabulary Notes: Units 01-04

Unit 01: Hello & Goodbye

Present Simple for facts. Verb 'to be' (am/is/are) and possessive adjectives (my, your, his, her).

Unit 02: Meeting someone new

Present Simple for personal info. Articles a/an. Prepositions (from, in, at).

Unit 03: Busy Lives

Adverbs of frequency (always, often, usually). Prepositions of time (at, in, on).

Unit 04: Take it easy!

Present Simple questions/negatives with do/does. Wh-questions (Where, What, When, Why).
