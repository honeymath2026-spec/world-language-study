Grammar & Vocabulary Notes: Units 09-12

Unit 09: What are you doing?

Present Continuous for actions in progress. Wh-questions (What are you doing?).

Unit 10: What are you going to do?

Going to for future plans. Will for predictions and spontaneous decisions.

Unit 11: I have to...

Have to / Has to for obligation. Must for strong obligation. Should for advice.

Unit 12: Comparing things

Comparative adjectives (-er, more). Superlative adjectives (-est, most).
