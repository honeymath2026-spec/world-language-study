Grammar & Vocabulary Notes: Units 05-08

Unit 05: Where do you live?

There is/are for existence. Prepositions of place (in, near, next to). Quantifiers (some, any, a lot of).

Unit 06: Can you speak Japanese?

Modal verb 'can' for ability. Past Simple of 'to be' (was/were). Infinitive with 'to' after verbs.

Unit 07: A Life of Learning

Past Simple (regular and irregular). Question formation with 'did'. Time expressions (ago, when, in).

Unit 08: How long ago?

Past Simple review. Question words (who, what, where). Time expressions (ago, in, when).
